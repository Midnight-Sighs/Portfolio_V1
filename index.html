About Midnight

Originally completely September 11th, 2021; V2 done (for now) September 16th

Robots VS Dinosaurs was our first project in python. It broke the high that I had been having up to this point because it was really difficult to wrap my head around the calling of classes and I kept getting confused. For the original date when I turned it in, I just got it functional and that was it.

However, what I turned in may have been technically correct and followed the user stories, but it was not up to my standards. Hence, V2.0 was born.

I know I could have just edited and updated the repo of the original but I wanted to keep it pure as a reminder of just how much I feel I improved it and how quickly I did so.
