About Midnight

Although I said this page was for my software development, I am much more than that. I am a jack-of-all-trades artist, a gamer (table-top and digital), and a mom of two boys. I am diverse and fun and I truly believe that we have to make our moments fun and I try to bring fun into things any way that I can, even if it's small.

I go by Midnight because I hate my real name (it's Sierra, for anyone who truly wants to use it) because I feel like it's never suited me. In the art world, I've also gone by the alias of Arreis-Kurai.
